Lasasgna

Description

Welcome to the ultimate comfort food experience! This classic lasagna recipe brings together layers of rich, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese to create the perfect hearty meal. Whether you're cooking for a cozy family dinner or preparing a feast to share with friends, this lasagna is sure to impress with its delicious flavors and satisfying texture. Grab your apron and let’s dive into the world of homemade goodness—your taste buds are in for a treat!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 475 g smooth ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Tomato Meat Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 onions diced
  • 1 rib celery diced
  • 1 carrot diced
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 11/2 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/3 cup tomato paste
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup read wine
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. In Dutch oven, heat oil over medium heat; cook onions, celery, carrot and garlic, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add beef; cook, breaking up with back of wooden spoon, until browned and liquid has evaporated, about 6 minutes.
  3. Stir in tomato paste. Add tomatoes, wine, bay leaves, oregano and pepper; simmer, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened, about 40 minutes. Stir in basil. Discard bay leaves.
  4. Meanwhile, in large pot of boiling salted water, cook noodles for 2 minutes less than package directions for al dente. Drain and arrange noodles in single layer on towels.
  5. In small bowl, stir together ricotta, eggs and pepper.
  6. In 13- x 9-inch (3 L) baking dish, spread 1-1/2 cups of the meat sauce. Arrange 3 noodles over top; sprinkle with half of the mozzarella cheese. Top with 2-1/2 cups of the meat sauce, 3 noodles, ricotta mixture, 3 noodles, 2-1/2 cups of the meat sauce, 3 noodles, then remaining meat sauce and mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
  7. Cover with foil. Bake in 375ºF (190ºC) oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake until cheese is golden, about 15 minutes. Loosely cover with foil and let stand for 30 minutes before serving.
